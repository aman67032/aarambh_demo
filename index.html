Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about Aarambha 2025.

üè† Campus Life & Facilities

What facilities are available on campus? +

Our campus offers state-of-the-art facilities including modern libraries with 24/7 access, computer labs with high-speed internet, sports complexes with gym facilities, cafeterias serving diverse cuisines, medical center with qualified staff, ATMs and banking services, stationery shops, and recreational areas for students to relax and socialize.

How do I get around campus? +

The campus provides shuttle services at regular intervals, bicycle rental services, and well-marked walking paths. Maps are available at the information desk and through our mobile app. Most academic buildings are within 10-15 minutes walking distance from the hostels.

What are the campus safety measures? +

We have 24/7 security personnel, CCTV surveillance across campus, well-lit pathways, emergency call boxes, and a dedicated campus safety app. There's also a night patrol service and escort services available upon request for late-night movement.

üìö Academics & Studies

How does the grading system work? +

We follow a comprehensive grading system with continuous assessment including assignments (20%), mid-term exams (30%), final exams (40%), and participation/attendance (10%). Letter grades range from A+ to F with corresponding GPA points. Detailed grading rubrics are provided for each course.

What academic support services are available? +

We offer tutoring centers for various subjects, writing assistance programs, study groups facilitated by senior students, academic counseling services, time management workshops, and specialized support for students with learning disabilities. Office hours with professors are also scheduled regularly.

How do I choose my courses and create a schedule? +

Course registration happens online through our student portal. You'll have an academic advisor assigned to help you plan your schedule. Prerequisites are clearly marked, and the system will alert you to any conflicts. Priority registration is given based on credit hours completed.

üè† Accommodation & Housing

What types of accommodation are available? +

We offer various housing options including single rooms, shared rooms (2-3 students), apartment-style housing for seniors, and specialized floors for international students. All rooms come with basic furniture, internet access, and utilities included. Some buildings offer air conditioning and premium amenities.

What are the hostel rules and regulations? +

Key rules include visitor policies (guests allowed till 10 PM with registration), noise restrictions after 11 PM, no smoking or alcohol in rooms, mandatory participation in fire drills, keeping common areas clean, and respecting roommate agreements. Detailed handbook will be provided during check-in.

How do laundry facilities work? +

Each hostel building has coin-operated washers and dryers on every floor. You can also use the mobile app to check machine availability and receive notifications when your cycle is complete. Commercial laundry services are also available for pickup and delivery.

üí∞ Fees & Financial Aid

What scholarships and financial aid are available? +

We offer merit-based scholarships, need-based grants, work-study programs, emergency financial assistance, and specialized scholarships for different fields of study. Applications are reviewed each semester, and students can also apply for external scholarships with our guidance.

How and when do I pay my fees? +

Fees can be paid online through the student portal, by bank transfer, or at designated payment centers. Payment deadlines are clearly communicated, and installment plans are available. Late payment fees may apply, but extensions can be requested through the finance office in special circumstances.

üçΩÔ∏è Dining & Meal Plans

What dining options are available on campus? +

We have multiple dining halls serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, food courts with various cuisines, coffee shops, convenience stores, and special dietary accommodation for students with allergies or religious dietary requirements.

How do meal plans work? +

Students can choose from various meal plans ranging from 10 meals per week to unlimited access. Meals are accessed using your student ID card, and unused meals can sometimes roll over. Flex dollars can be used at campus eateries and convenience stores.

üéØ Orientation Week

What happens during Aarmbha 2025 orientation week? +

Orientation includes campus tours, academic advising sessions, social activities and ice-breakers, information sessions about resources and services, club and organization fairs, welcome ceremony, technology setup assistance, and sessions on college life skills and safety.

Is attendance mandatory for all orientation activities? +

While most activities are highly recommended, some sessions like academic advising and safety briefings are mandatory. Social activities are optional but strongly encouraged as they help you meet fellow students and build connections that will enhance your college experience.
